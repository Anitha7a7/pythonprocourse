Title: Flatmates Bill
Description : An app that gets the details of Flatmates along with the
no: of days they have stayed in the house and the Bill amount to return the splitted Bill amount in between
them. It also generates an PDF document stating the names of the flatmates with their respective bill amounts
to be paid.

Objects:
Bill - amount, period
Flatmate - name, days_in_house, pays(bill)
PdfReport - filename, generate(flatemate1, flatmate2, bill)
